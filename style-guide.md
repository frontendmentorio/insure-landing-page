## Typography

### Body Copy

- Font size: 16px

### Headings

- Family: [DM Serif Display](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Serif+Display)
- Weights: 400

### Body

- Family: [Karla](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Karla)
- Weights: 400, 700
